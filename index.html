aquaponics?

a self sustainable system where plants and fish co-exist organically.
the water with nutrients from the fish is used by the plants, and the water from the plants flows back to the fish
this balance creates a holistic self-sustainable system.

why choose aquaponics?

  • healthier & tastier produce
  • more produce, less space benefits of vertically stacked system
  • uses 92% less water than traditional practices
  • self-sufficient, self-cleaning fish tanks (no fishy smell!)
  • much cleaner, no harmful pesticides
  • extremely simple to maintain
  • multi-cropping is a breeze
